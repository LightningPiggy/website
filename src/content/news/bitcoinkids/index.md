Here is a collection of fantastic educational projects for children of all ages designed to supercharge their skills in science, technology, engineering, maths, and money. If you are an educator focused on Bitcoin and freedom technology and would like to be included on this list, please get in [touch](mailto:oink@lightningpiggy.com).

## Education Hubs

### [Bitcoin Junior Club](https://x.com/BTCJuniorClub)

Education-fun zone for kids, teens & parents alike. All things #Bitcoin in one place.

### [EconoKidz](https://www.bitcoin4youth.com)

An animated course that introduces children to essential economic concepts, money, and Bitcoin. Perfect as a supplement to any homeschool or traditional school curriculum, EconoKidz is designed for kids aged 8 and up, parents, teachers, or anyone interested in these fascinating topics. Narrated by two young Bitcoiners aged 8 and 9, this free resource is both engaging and educational.

## Entrepreneurial Inspiration

### [Bee Happy Farm](https://beehappyfarm.co.uk)

Nineveh is a 9-year-old who is home-educated and lives in a lovely house with a spacious garden full of animals. She cares for three cats, two goats, 18 chickens, two ducks, and about 50,000 bees! With a deep passion for animals, Nineveh aspires to become a veterinarian. At Bee Happy Farm, she and her helpers offer child-friendly experience days where they provide tours and share fascinating information about all the animals.
